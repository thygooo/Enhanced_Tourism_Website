IBAYAW CHATBOT ROLE-BASED TEST PACK (PILOT TEST)
Date: 2026-03-25
Scope: Chatbot intent coverage by role + multilingual behavior

How to use:
1) Log in with a role account (guest / owner / admin / employee).
2) Open chatbot and send prompts below exactly.
3) Mark PASS if expected behavior happens.
4) Save screenshots for thesis appendix/demo.

==================================================
A) GUEST TESTS
==================================================
A1. "recommend a hotel in bayawan for 2 guests under 2000"
Expected: Accommodation recommendations from DB; no invented records.

A2. "kinahanglan kog inn sa villareal para sa 3 ka bisita, budget 1200"
Expected: Understood via translation; recommendations or clear clarifying question.

A3. "show tourism information about bayawan city"
Expected: Tourism info from published DB records only.

A4. "book room 111 for 2 guests from 2026-04-10 to 2026-04-12"
Expected: Booking draft/confirmation flow; guest-linked booking only.

A5. "calculate hotel bill for room 111 for 2 nights"
Expected: Billing response from backend logic.

A6. "recommend tours for 2 guests under 1000"
Expected: Tour recommendations from published schedules.

A7. After tour recommendations, send: "book #1"
Expected: Returns clickable booking link/button for selected tour schedule.

A8. "show my bookings"
Expected: Button/link opens guest booking page in new tab.
